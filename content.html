Introduction

Laboratory courses for hands-on experience are integral part of engineering education. The Solid State Devices(SSD) Virtual Laboratory is an initiative to develop virtual instrument based online Devices and circuits Laboratory covering most of the experiments suggested in the AICTE Model Syllabi for all the AICTE approved Engineering colleges in India - offering undergraduate courses in electrical, electronics, electronics and communications, instrumentation, computer science and IT. Currently, more than 15 experiments on electronics devices and circuits are available for the undergraduate students. The Lab enables students to visualize various semiconductor devices and circuits at any time and from anywhere using a web browser. The SSD Virtual Laboratory is a complete solutions for teachers for teaching the devices and circuits laboratory classes with students authentication remote experiments,submission of online laboratory reports, holding test and viva-voce examinations and fully grading the students and also preparing the grade sheet for the class, SSD Virtual Laboratory developed for teaching undergraduate laboratory classes - is available to a Engi- neering colleges in India
